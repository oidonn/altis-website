FAQ

Common Questions

How is this different from an accountant or bookkeeper?

Accountants and bookkeepers focus on recordkeeping, taxes, and compliance. We focus on why profitability is changing, where margin is leaking, and what operational decisions can improve performance.

What information do you need to get started?

Most engagements start with:

→Last 12 months of P&Ls
→Payroll summaries
→POS sales exports
→Vendor or purchasing reports (if available)
What if our books aren't clean?

That's common. Perfect books are not required. Our process is built to work with real-world operator data and identify actionable trends even when reporting is imperfect.

We already have a GM or operations manager — why would we need this?

Your GM runs the business day-to-day. We provide an independent financial and operational layer focused on cross-location performance, margin variance, and cash flow optimization — the layer your GM typically doesn't have time for.

What happens after the diagnostic?

You receive a full findings report and 90-day action plan. If implementation support is needed, optional advisory services may be available on a case-by-case basis.
